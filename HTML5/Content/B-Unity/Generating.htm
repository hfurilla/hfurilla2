Generating Captions in Sharepoint Stream

When it comes to making your own videos through applications like Sharepoint Stream, it’s key that you find a way to incorporate captions or transcripts so any viewer can easily view your content. Thankfully, Sharepoint Stream has a feature built in that allows you to make your videos accessible.

  1. First, record and edit your video. When you’ve reached the final step, a preview of your video will appear along with a sidebar titled Video Settings.

  2. In Video Settings, click the Transcript and Captions option. The setting expands. Here you can choose to upload a transcript of your video as a WebVTT (vtt.) file, or generate captions.

  3. For the purpose of this tutorial, click Generate. The button disappears and the text Generating… appears in its place as the program processes the audio.

  4. To edit any mistranslations of the text, hover over the section you’d like to edit then click the Edit button. Your cursor now appears in the transcript.

  5. Retype and edit the text as you see fit.
